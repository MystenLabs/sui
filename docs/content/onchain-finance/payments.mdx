Integrate payment flows into your application, from reading and managing address balances to sponsoring gasless transactions for your users.

:::info Regulatory considerations

Payment applications may be subject to KYC, AML, and money-transmitter licensing requirements depending on your jurisdiction and business model. These requirements are outside the scope of Sui documentation. Consult qualified legal counsel before launching a payment product.

:::
