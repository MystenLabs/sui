Module reader 

Source
Expand description

Adapter that exposes RpcStoreSchema through the trait stack sui-rpc-api consumes — ObjectStore, ReadStore, ChildObjectResolver, RpcStateReader, and RpcIndexes.

The adapter type, RpcStoreReader, is generic over a Reader so a single struct serves both tip reads (R = Db) and point-in-time reads bound to a snapshot (R = Snapshot). Callers requesting “give me the latest” hold the tip reader; callers requesting “show me state at checkpoint X” hold the snapshot-bound one. The choice of consistency context is made once, at the point RpcStoreReader::at_snapshot is called.
